# Koonafa Global - Official Website

🌍 **Global Hospitality Marketing & Tourism Representation**

Comprehensive hospitality representation across UAE, India, Middle East, South East Asia, and Africa.

## 🏨 About Koonafa Global

Koonafa Global represents the full hospitality ecosystem—luxury island resorts, downtown hotels, restaurants, tour operators, DMCs, TMCs, and tourism boards. We bridge premium brands with high-value markets through strategic B2B partnerships and commercial representation.

## 🚀 Website Features

- **Auto-Rotating Hero Carousel** - Showcasing luxury destinations
- **6 Hospitality Sectors** - Interactive cards with hover intelligence
- **Specialized Services** - Comprehensive representation capabilities
- **Partner Segments** - Targeted support across 9 verticals
- **Travel Trade Associations** - Scrolling marquee of industry affiliations
- **Mobile Responsive** - Optimized for all devices
